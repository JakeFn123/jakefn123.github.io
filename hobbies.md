# Interests

I want this page to show the parts of life that shape how I think and work, not just what appears on a resume.

## Technology as Craft

I enjoy the part of technology that feels like craft: shaping tools carefully, improving workflows, refining interfaces, and noticing the difference between something that merely works and something that feels right.

## Writing and Reflection

Writing helps me turn experience into understanding. I use notes, drafts, and essays to clarify what I believe, what I am learning, and what I want to build next.

## Systems and Aesthetics

I am drawn to systems that are both useful and elegant. That might mean software architecture, a clean working process, or a website that feels intentional instead of generic.
