<div class="blk-v2">
  <div class="sh-v2">Research Overview</div>
  <p style="color:#3d5060;line-height:1.75;max-width:680px;">My PhD research at Tongji University sits at the intersection of intelligent agent systems, machine learning, and engineering optimization — with a focus on making AI systems practically useful for complex, real-world engineering and research tasks. I work under Prof. Qi Kang in the School of Electronic and Information Engineering.</p>
</div>

<div class="blk-v2">
  <div class="sh-v2">Current Research Directions</div>
  <div class="ri-grid">
    <div class="ri-card c-blue">
      <div class="ri-title">Agentic AI Systems</div>
      <p>Designing multi-agent architectures with memory, planning, and tool-use for complex task execution. Focus on research automation, human-AI collaboration, and reproducible workflows.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="ri-card c-violet">
      <div class="ri-title">Continual / Incremental Learning</div>
      <p>Class incremental learning with feature knowledge prompts — enabling models to acquire new knowledge without catastrophic forgetting of previous tasks. Published at WOCC 2025.</p>
    </div>
    <div class="ri-card c-teal">
      <div class="ri-title">Engineering Optimization</div>
      <p>Intelligent algorithms (evolutionary, metaheuristic, learning-based) for combinatorial optimization in manufacturing — flexible job-shop scheduling, process planning, materials optimization.</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
